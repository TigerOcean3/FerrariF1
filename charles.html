Charles Leclerc

Scuderia Ferrari F1 Driver




About Charles

Charles Leclerc is a Monégasque racing driver who competes in Formula One for Scuderia Ferrari. He made his debut in F1 in 2018, and has rapidly become one of the most talented and promising drivers of his generation. Known for his speed, consistency, and incredible racecraft, Charles is one of the brightest stars in Formula 1.




“Ever since I was a kid, my most heartfelt dream was to be able to drive the red car and I've managed to make it come true.”
